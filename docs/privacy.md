# Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-05-11.

Masquerade does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The
app runs entirely on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no crash
reporting, and no third-party SDKs that send data off the device.

## Permissions

- **Camera** — used only when you open the QR scanner. Camera frames are
  processed on-device and discarded after each scan. No image data is stored
  or transmitted.

## Local storage

Preferences (theme, history of recent inputs) are stored on your device using
the system preferences API. They are never synced or transmitted. Clearing the
app's data, or deleting the app, removes them.
